About Me

I am superhero with the ability to manipulate temperature and create ice. I uses various physical processes to achieve my powers.

  • Powers

    My powers are based on cryogenics and thermodynamic processes. I can use my powers to lower the temperature of objects and the surrounding air, which can cause them to freeze. I can also create ice out of thin air, and I use this ice to create a variety of objects, such as shields, weapons, and platforms.

  • Conduction

    I can speed up or magnify the conduction processes, which is the transfer of heat from one object to another. This allows him to freeze objects quickly.

  • Convection

    I can speed up the process of cold air sinking (due to density) and warm air rising

  • Magnetism

    I can use magnetism to lower the temperature of objects. Successive magnetization and demagnetization under the proper combination of conditions can lower the temperature to only about a millionth of a degree above absolute zero.

  • Pressure

    I can can control pressure to lower the temperature of substances

  • The Freezer Process

    I can emit a refrigerant, such as cooled helium, which lowers the temperature of the surrounding air. This allows me to create a protective shield of ice.

  • The Freezer Process P2 (Cryogenics)

    In order to emit cooled gases, I have the power to lower the temperature of gases using cryogenic processes inside my body.

    I have a variety of gasses I can cool through internal air compression
